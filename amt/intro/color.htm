Understanding Color Accuracy & Visibility

This attribute measures how natural and accurate the colors appear in the video. Please watch the following examples to understand the different levels:

Colors look natural - Example 1
This example shows a video with natural-looking colors. The colors appear true to life with proper balance and saturation.
Colors look natural - Example 2
Another example of natural color reproduction, showing well-balanced and accurate colors throughout the scene.
Colors look slightly off - Example 1
This example shows colors that are slightly unnatural. You may notice some color tinting or slight oversaturation.

Another example of slightly unnatural colors, with noticeable color shifts in certain areas of the scene.

This example shows severely unnatural colors. The video appears heavily tinted or has extreme color shifts.
