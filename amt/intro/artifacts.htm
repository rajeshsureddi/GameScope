Understanding Pixelation & Blockiness

This attribute measures the presence of compression artifacts and blocky patterns in the video. Please watch the following examples to understand the different levels:

No visible artifacts - Example 1
This example shows a video with no visible compression artifacts. The image appears smooth and natural without any blocky patterns.
No visible artifacts - Example 2
Another example of a video with no visible compression artifacts, demonstrating smooth and natural image quality.
Minor Artifacts Present - Example 1
This example shows some minor compression artifacts. You may notice slight blockiness in areas with high motion or detail.
Minor Artifacts Present - Example 2
Another example showing minor compression artifacts, particularly noticeable during fast-paced scenes.
Severe Artifacts Present - Example 1
This example shows severe compression artifacts. The video appears heavily pixelated with obvious blocky patterns throughout.
Severe Artifacts Present - Example 2
Another example of severe compression artifacts, showing significant pixelation and blockiness across the entire video.
