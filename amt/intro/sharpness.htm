Understanding Clarity & Sharpness

This attribute measures how clear and sharp the video appears. Please watch the following examples to understand the different levels:

Highly Sharp and Clear - Example 1
This example shows a video with excellent clarity and sharpness. All details are clearly visible with crisp edges and no blurriness.
Highly Sharp and Clear - Example 2
Another example of excellent clarity and sharpness, with perfect focus and detail throughout the scene.
Moderately Sharp and Clear - Example 1
This example shows acceptable clarity and sharpness. While not perfect, the video is still clear enough to see most details.
Moderately Sharp and Clear - Example 2
Another example of moderate clarity, where the image is generally clear but with some areas of reduced sharpness.
Minimally Sharp and Clear - Example 1
This example shows poor clarity and sharpness. The video appears blurry with unclear details and soft edges.
Minimally Sharp and Clear - Example 2
Another example of poor clarity, with significant blurriness and lack of detail throughout the video.
